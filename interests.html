Reverse engineering

Reverse engineering is process through which one tries to understand the workings of certain software or hardware in case of IT. It could involve disassembling the program or testing it, trying to recreate the source code.

Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a broad field of IT with many definitions. It focuses on resolving complex problems, usually ones that can't be resolved quickly using normal algorithms.Some approaches to ai involve decision trees, different heuristic algorithms and when there aren't any other options also machine learning including neural networks.
